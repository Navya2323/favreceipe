Pineapple Kesari

Ingredients

  • 200g Rava
  • 100ml Oil
  • 100 Ghee
  • 50g cashews
  • 50g Drygrapes
  • 250g Sugar
  • Color Powered
  • 1 tbsp Essence

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a vessel boil the water,add the rava
  2. Add sugar and stir
  3. Add food color and Essence
  4. Finally heat the oil and ghee in medium heat, Add the cashews and drygrapes, Remove from heat.
  5. Serve immediately with love
